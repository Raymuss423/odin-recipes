Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Pasta Bake

This Cajun shrimp and sausage pasta bake is fast to assemble featuring andouille sausage and shrimp in a creamy Cajun Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 8 ounces shrimp, peeled and de-veined
  • 8 ounces andouille sauasge, quartered

Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 cup 2% milk
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water
  • 4 ounces garlic and herb spreadable cheese, such as alouette garlic and herb soft spreadable cheese
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat the ovento 375 degrees F. Grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a heavy skillet. Add onion and bell pepper, and cook until softened, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in sausage and shrimp. Turn off heat; residual heat will cook the shrimp and warm the sausage. Remoce skillet from heat after a few minutes.
  3. Stir penne into boiling water and return to boil. Cook past uncovered, stirring occasionally, for 6 minutes.
  4. Using a slotted spoon, transfer about half of pasta to the skillet. Transfer remaining pasta to prepared casserole. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  5. In a saucepan, melt 1 tblespoon butter over medium heat and whisk in flour. Add garlic and Cajun seasoning. Whisking constantly, slowly pour in milk, half and half, and reserved pasta water until sauce is smooth and bubbly, 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in Parmesan cheese and allow it to melt, 1 to 2 minutes more.
  6. Pour half of sauce over pasta in the casserole. Transfer sausage mixture to the casserole,a nd use a spoon to gently blend all ingredients. Pour remaining sauce over pasta mixture. Cover casserole with foil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and bubbly, about 25 minutes. Serve immediately.
